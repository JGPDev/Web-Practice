We all love snickerdooles!

My family loves when I make some. I personally like them a bit on the soft side.

How do you like your snickerdoodles?

Ingredients:

  • 2 sticks of butter.
  • 1 TBsp of Vanilla extract.
  • 1 cup of white sugar.
  • 1/3 cup of brown sugar.
  • 2 eggs.
  • 3 cups of all-purpose flower.
  • 2 tsp of cinnamon.
  • 2 tsp cream of tartar.
      • Pro tip: Use 1 tsp of lemon juice!
  • 2 tsb of baking soda (I personally use 1).
  • 1/2 tsb of salt.
